Driver Licence Number (master number)

Your driver licence number is made up of 3 sets of letters and numbers

  1. The first set of letters is the first 5 letters of your last name (or less if your name is shorter).
  2. The second set of numbers is your birth date entered numericly by Day Month Year (For example DD MM YY).
  3. The last set of numbers are 3 numbers that are unique to you and can be found on your driver licence.
Review the first 5 letters of your last name and birthdate then enter the last three numbers of your driver licence number
  1. First 5 letters or less
  2. DD MM YY
  3. 3 numbers

Example of a driver licence number on your licence

You can find the information you need printed on the front of your licence, labeled with 4d.
